Dating Simulator

The project was made during a hackathon called "Nuit de l'info" in 2023.
The objective was to make an interactive website to motivate people to respect climate change.
We were a team of 9 people, and we worked on the project for 16 hours.
There were some challenges that we had to accomplish, such as:
- There must be a hidden konami code in the website.
- The website must be 5 themes in our case it was (hell, winter, love, konami, and default).
